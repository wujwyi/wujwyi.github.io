About Me

My name is Jiayi Wu and I am a PhD student at the School of Data Science and Engineering, East China Normal University. My research interests include natural language processing, pre-trained language models, deep learning, and brain-computer interfaces.

Apart from my academic pursuits, I have a passion for DIY desktop computers and I am highly interested in computer hardware. I also enjoy racing simulation games and am a fan of F1 racing, especially the Ferrari F1 team.

Education

  • PhD in Data Science and Engineering, East China Normal University (2022 - Present)
  • Bachelor of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University of Technology (2018 - 2022)

Selected Publications

  • Tang, Z., Wang, X., Wu, J., Ping, Y., Guo, X., & Cui, Z. (2022). A BCI painting system using hybrid control based on SSVEP and P300. Computers in Biology and Medicine.

Selected Awards and Achievements

  • National First Prize, 2020 National College Student Mathematical Modeling Competition
  • Finalist, 2021 MCM/ICM International Student Mathematical Modeling Competition
